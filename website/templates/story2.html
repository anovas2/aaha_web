Where is the rent burden?, 2014-2018
Rent burdened household relative to renter occupied units
  • Looking at this map reveals that, in 2016 alone, low-income, rent-burdened and severely rent-burdened households were concentrated in the southern portion of Metro ATL, while some moderate to higher-income renters experienced rent burden and severe rent burden, in less volume, in a few portions of the northern portion of Metro ATL.
  • The increased shading on the map is proportionate to the increased volume of renter-occupied units concentrated in a census tract...but, are there enough affordable units relative to the incomes of residents in those areas?
